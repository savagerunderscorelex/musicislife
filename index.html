music is life

I created this website as a tribute to my favorite music artists and bands. I've always wanted to share my music taste with more people, especially since the music artists I listen to would be considered "obscure." Through this website, I hope to share underrated artists and bands people might like but would have never heard about otherwise.

Originally, I had about 50 artists on this website, but during development, I decided to lower this number to 5, then 3. I did this because my skills were still lacking, so I'd rather focus on understanding how I would create this site before creating more and more pages.

Each page contains a short artist description, a video, and 4 vinyls your can click on to play a song from that artist. Click on the vinyls to play them, and you can also pause the music by just clicking them again. That's it. :)
